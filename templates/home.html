Welcome to JEE Main

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main is one of the most important engineering entrance exams in India.

About JEE Main

The JEE Main exam is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate engineering programs at various IITs, NITs, and other engineering colleges in India.

Eligibility Criteria

To appear for JEE Main, candidates must have completed or be appearing in their 10+2 exams (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.

Exam Pattern

JEE Main is a computer-based test consisting of multiple-choice questions (MCQs) in two papers: Paper 1 (for B.E./B.Tech) and Paper 2 (for B.Arch/B.Planning). The exam duration is 3 hours, and the maximum score is 300 marks.

Important Dates

The exam is typically held twice a year (January and April). The exact dates are announced by the NTA on the official website. Make sure to check the dates for the upcoming session.
